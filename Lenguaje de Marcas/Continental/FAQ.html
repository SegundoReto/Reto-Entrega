FAQ

FAQ – frequently asked questions

What is the check-in & check- in time limits?
Your room will be available from 3 p.m. In case of morning arrival, it is possible to leave all your suitcases in our storage room, for free. On your departure day you can keep your room up to noon. We will be happy to keep your luggage after your check out.
What are the reception opening times?
The reception is open 24h a day, 7 days a week
Do you have a Spa & Fitness area?
We offer you a spa area which includes a sauna, a steam room and a fitness room. This wellness area is open daily from 6am to 22h, upon reservation of a 45 minutes session (free for all our hotel guests). We also propose Massages on reservation.
How to get here?
By car:
Reaching the hotel by car is easy: it's a short drive from the Boulevard Périphérique orbital road. Travelling clockwise, come off at Avenue de la Porte d'Asnières, then turn right onto Boulevard des Maréchaux until you reach Avenue Gourgaud. Continue along this street and Avenue Niel to Rue des Acacias. Travelling anticlockwise, leave the Périphérique at Rue Gustave Charpentier, turn left onto Avenue des Ternes and right onto Rue des Acacias at Avenue Mac-Mahon. You can park your car at one of 3 public car parks within 5 minutes' walk of the hotel.

By Train:
Arriving by train? The hotel is easy to reach from the city's mainline railway stations. Taxis typically take 20 to 40 minutes, depending on traffic. By metro you can reach the hotel from Gare du Nord via lines 4 and 2. Gare de Lyon is on a direct metro route, line 1. From Gare d'Austerlitz, you travel 2 stops on line 5 before transferring to line 1. From Gare de l'Est, board line 7 and change to line 1, or catch bus 30. Line 6 links Gare Montparnasse with Charles de Gaulle Etoile station, near the hotel.

By Plane:
If you're flying into Paris, reaching the hotel is simple. Taxis to the hotel from Paris-Charles de Gaulle Airport (CDG) take around 35 to 50 minutes. From Paris-Orly Airport (ORY), it's a 30- to 45-minute taxi ride. Le-Bus Direct runs buses from both airports to Place Charles de Gaulle (Place de l'Etoile). From Charles de Gaulle Airport, you can also hop on RER B and change at Châtelet Les Halles to metro line 1, getting off at Charles de Gaulle Etoile station. In addition, hotel staff will be delighted to arrange a direct shuttle on request.
